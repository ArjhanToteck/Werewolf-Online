Lost Mason

Lost masons are masons separated from their secret society. Every night, they can check if a player is a mason. If they are a mason, the lost mason will be reunited with the others and have access to the mason chat at night. If the person they check isn't a mason, they will have to check someone else the next day.

Additional Commands

  • Every night, use the command !check (username) to check the if a player is a mason. If they are, you will be able to talk to all the other masons and will no longer have this command.

    • Role Type

  • Lost masons are members of the village faction.
  • Lost masons are seen as members of the village by the seer.
