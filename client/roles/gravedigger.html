Gravedigger

Gravediggers are villagers that can check the role of a dead player every night.

Additional Commands

  • Every night, use the command !check (username) to check the dead of the chosen dead player. The moderator will tell you what the faction is in the morning.

    • Role Type

  • Gravediggers are members of the village faction.
  • Gravediggers are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • Gravediggers are very useful when a player claims to have evidence of another player being a werewolf. If the accused was lynched, you should verify they were a wolf. If they weren't, somebody was lying...
  • If there is a healer in the game, you can help them pick the right target to heal.
