Vigilante

Vigilantes are villagers that have the ability to kill another player during the night once per game.

Additional Commands

  • Once per game and during the night, use the command !kill (username) to kill the chosen player.

    • Role Type

  • Vigilantes are members of the village faction.
  • Vigilantes are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • Save your shot until you are convinced you know the identity of a werewolf. You only have one shot.
