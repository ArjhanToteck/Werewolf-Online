Protector

Protectors are villagers that can protect players, including themselves, from being killed during the night.

Additional Commands

  • Every night, use the command !protect (username) to protect the chosen player. You can protect yourself. You cannot protect the same person two nights in a row. You cannot protect players from dying under certain circumstances such as an old man dying from natural causes or an avenger bringing somebody to die with them.

    • Role Type

  • Protectors are members of the village faction.
  • Protectors are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • It's your job to protect the powerful players from the wolves. You have to think like a wolf to know who they will try to kill so you know who to protect.
