Villager

The villager is the most common role in Werewolf. A villager has no special abilities and instead must use nothing but the information they can deduce to figure out who the werewolves are and vote them off.

Role Type

  • Villagers are members of the village faction.
  • Villagers are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • Just because you don't have any special abilities does not mean you're useless.
  • Don't wait for players with special abilities to save you. Try to figure things out yourself and be proactive.
