Wolf Cub

Wolf Cubs, like all other members of the wolfpack, have the ability to communicate privately with other wolfpack members at night and kill another player every night, though only one member of the wolfpack can kill per night. Additionally, if a wolf cub dies, the wolf pack gets an extra kill that night.

Additional Commands

  • During the night, use the command !kill (username) command to take the wolfpack kill and murder the chosen player.

    • Role Type

  • Wolf cubs are members of the wolfpack faction.
  • Wolf cubs are seen as members of the wolfpack by the seer.

    • Tips

  • As a wolf cub, you can plan with the other wolves to try to get yourself killed so you can get an extra kill the next night.
