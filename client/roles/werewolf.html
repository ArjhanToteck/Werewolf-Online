Werewolf

Werewolves, like all other members of the wolfpack, have the ability to communicate privately with other wolfpack members at night and kill another player every night, though only one member of the wolfpack can kill per night.

Additional Commands

  • During the night, use the command !kill (username) command to take the wolfpack kill and murder the chosen player.

    • Role Type

  • Werewolves are members of the wolfpack faction.
  • Werewolves are seen as members of the wolfpack by the seer.

    • Tips

  • Try not to show too much of an alliance with the other werewolves during the day. This type of thing gets suspicious fast.
