Baker

Bakers are villagers that can bake bread and give it to another player. That player will know they are a baker, and by extension, a village member.

Additional Commands

  • Once per game and during the night, use the command !give (username) to give bread to the chosen player.

    • Role Type

  • Bakers are members of the village faction.
  • Bakers are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • Bakers are a powerful role so giving bread to a werewolf will most likely get you killed.
