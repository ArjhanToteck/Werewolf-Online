Fool

Fools are regular villagers that are convinced they are seers. Note that the game will tell any player who has this role that they are a seer.

Additional Commands

  • Every night, use the command !check (username) to check the faction of the chosen player. Since you aren't an actual seer, and rather a fool, the information given is no good.

    • Role Type

  • Fools are members of the village faction.
  • Fools are seen as members of the village by the seer.
