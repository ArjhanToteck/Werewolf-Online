Martyr

Martyr are villagers that can sacrifice their own lives to protect other from being killed during the night.

Additional Commands

  • Every night, use the command !protect (username) to protect the chosen player. You cannot protect yourself. You cannot protect the same person two nights in a row. You cannot protect players from dying under certain circumstances such as an old man dying from natural causes or an avenger bringing somebody to die with them. If somebody tries to kill the user you are protecting, you will die instead of them, which protection cannot prevent.

    • Role Type

  • Martyrs are members of the village faction.
  • Martyrs are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • It's your job to protect the powerful players from the wolves. You have to think like a wolf to know who they will try to kill so you know who to protect.
