Doppelgänger

Doppelgängers are a temporary role who on the first night copy the role of another player and keep that role for the rest of the game.

Additional Commands

  • On the first night, use the command !copy (username) to copy the role of another player. This role will be yours for the rest of the game. If you don't use this command on the first night, you will copy the role of a random player.

    • Role Type

  • Doppelgängers are members of the village faction.
  • Doppelgängers are seen as members of the village by the seer.
