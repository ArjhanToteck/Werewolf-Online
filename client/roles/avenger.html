Avenger

Avengers are villagers with fire in their souls. When an avenger dies, they also kill the person that killed them, which protection from a protector or martyr cannot prevent. If they were voted off, a random person who voted for them will die.

Role Type

  • Avengers are members of the village faction.
  • Avengers are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • One stratefy for an avenger is to become a target. If you subtly hint that you are a powerful village role like a seer, the wolves will want to kill you, and as a result you will kill a wolf as well.
