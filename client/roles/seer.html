Seer

Seers are villagers that can check the faction of another living player every night.

Additional Commands

  • Every night, use the command !check (username) to check the faction of the chosen player. The moderator will tell you what the faction is in the morning.

    • Role Type

  • Seers are members of the village faction.
  • Seers are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • Remember, your check will be wrong if the player you checked is a lycan or if they were marked by the bloodletter.
  • The werewolves will kill a seer any chance they get. Do not reveal you are a seer unless you have crucial information.
  • Even though the game might say you are a seer, you could be a fool and all the checks you make could be false. Be aware of that possibility.
