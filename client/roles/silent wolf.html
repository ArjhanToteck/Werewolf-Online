Silent Wolf

Silent wolves, like all other members of the wolfpack, have the ability to communicate privately with other wolfpack members at night and kill another player every night, though only one member of the wolfpack can kill per night. What makes silent wolves special is that they are undetectable from a regular villager by seers.

Additional Commands

  • During the night, use the command !kill (username) command to take the wolfpack kill and murder the chosen player.

    • Role Type

  • Silent wolves are members of the wolfpack faction.
  • Silent wolves are seen as members of the village by the seer.

    • Tips

  • You can verify yourself as a villager by having the seer check you.
